BMI Calculator

A BMI Calculator helps determine your body mass index to assess if you're underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese based on your height and weight.

BMI Weight Status

Calculate your BMI

Calculate your BMI by dividing your weight in kilograms by the square of your height in meters to evaluate your body fat level.
